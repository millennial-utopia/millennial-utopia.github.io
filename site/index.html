Millennial Utopia / Home

This site hosts a collection of writing and data about a hypothetical social, economic, and political system collectively referred to as the Millennial Utopia. The name reflects the belief that this system could be achievable in human society with the tools available to us since the early third millennium.

The site is divided into two main sections:

Theory

Foundations and proposals for the structure of the MU, including a discussion of its basic principles and a roadmap for real-world implementation.

A full listing of all of the theory pages currently written can be found here.

Lemontopia

Stories about fictional micro-society that explores MU concepts in action, set on a small island with a particular fondness for a certain citrus fruit.

More details about the Lemontopians and a timeline can be found here.
