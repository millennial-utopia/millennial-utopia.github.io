Millennial Utopia / Home

The world we inherited from the last millennium is broken. But we already have the tools to build something better.

Millennial Utopia is a project exploring how we might construct a more just, sustainable, and livable society using technologies and knowledge already at our disposal.

The site is divided into two main sections:

Theory

Foundations and proposals for the structure of the MU, including a discussion of its basic principles and a roadmap for real-world implementation.

Lemontopia

A collection of short stories about a fictional agrarian micro-society with a curious fondness for a certain citrus fruit. These stories trace the evolution of this society from its theocratic roots to the centennial of its unification under a post-scarcity system inspired by the Millennial Utopian model. Along the way, the stories explore how this society was able to meet a variety of social, political, and economic challenges with limited resources.
